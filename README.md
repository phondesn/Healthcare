# MedFit-Assistant-App

## Description
<p>
MedFit Assistant, an app developed for the people of age above 65. A user friendly and simple app developed for
keeping the records of the medical as well as fitness details so that users can keep check their health.
</p>
<p>
The users can add the record of the reports as well as daily schedule of their medicine and exercise. It will help them
check which medicines are currently going on and which were taken in past. It will also helps to keep eye on medical
history of user.
</p>
<p>
The doctors appointment can be book from the list of top rated doctors by checking their names and addresses for the
different illnesses as well as fitness checkups.
</p>
<p>A 'MySQL' for database, 'Java (Springboot)' for backend and 'ReactJs' for frontend are all used in the development of the application.</p>
